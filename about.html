About Us

Welcome to GoalSeat. This project was created to help users browse football matches and request tickets in a simple and clear way.

Our website allows users to:

  • View available football matches
  • Check ticket prices and categories
  • Use simple login and signup forms
  • Move easily between the pages

Our goal is to make football ticket booking easier and more organized.

Thank you for using GoalSeat!
