About Us

Welcome to GoalSeat (our CPIS-358 assignment)! We created this website in order to help our users easily browse football matches as well as request tickets to go watch the matches live, in a simple and smooth manner!

Our goal is to provide a simple and clean interface that allows users to:

  • View all available football matches and their live scores
  • Check out ticket prices and categories
  • Help users buy tickets in the easiest way possible
  • Review match details clearly and efficiently all in one place

We believe that organizing football ticket information in one place helps users save time and makes the booking process a lot easier to understand and navigate through.

Thank you for using GoalSeat!
